About Aswani
Introduction
I am Ph.D. Candidate at the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, working on the applications of ICT, GIS, and Softcomputing to improve land consolidation activities in the state of Uttar Pradesh, India. My research interests are land acquisition, land administration, IT adoption in land administration, blockchain in the land records management, land informatics, land consolidation, land valuation, land rights, land survey/re-survey and settlement activities.
